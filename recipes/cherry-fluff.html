Cherry Fluff

Ingredients

  1. 1 (20 ounce) can crushed pineapple, drained
  2. 1 (21 ounce) can cherry pie filling
  3. 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  4. 1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed

Directions

Step 1

Mix crushed pineapple, cherry pie filling, sweetened condensed milk, and whipped topping together in a bowl; chill in the refrigerator at least 2 hours before serving.
